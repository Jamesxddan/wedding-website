# banner-design

Multi-format banner creation. 22+ styles, print and digital.

## Workflow (5 Steps)

1. **Gather requirements** — dimensions, format, brand, CTA, audience
2. **Research** — Pinterest inspiration + activate ui-ux-pro-max / frontend-design / ai-artist / ai-multimodal
3. **Generate** — HTML/CSS banner + AI images
4. **Export** — PNG via chrome-devtools screenshot
5. **Present** — Side-by-side variants for selection

## Design Rules

- Safe zone: central 70-80% of canvas
- Text: less than 20% of ad space
- Typefaces: max 2 per banner
- Contrast: minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- CTA button: single, minimum 44px touch target

## Print Specs

- 300 DPI minimum
- CMYK color space
- 3-5mm bleeds on all sides

## Styles (22+)

Bold, minimal, editorial, luxury, playful, corporate, retro, gradient, typographic, photographic, illustrative, neon, organic, brutalist, and more.

## Usage

Invoke for any banner, ad, or marketing asset creation. Specify dimensions, format (digital/print), brand guidelines, and CTA. Outputs HTML/CSS with export-ready PNG.
